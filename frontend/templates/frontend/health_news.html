1 - Mpox Cases Reported in Nairobi

Health officials have confirmed new cases of mpox in Nairobi, urging residents to seek testing and vaccination at local health centers. The Ministry of Health is deploying rapid response teams to contain the outbreak.

2 - Cholera Vaccination Drive in Coastal Region

A mass cholera vaccination campaign has been launched in Mombasa and Kilifi counties following recent flooding. Health workers are targeting vulnerable populations to curb the spread of the disease.

3 - Social Health Authority (SHA) Fully Operational

The Social Health Authority has officially replaced the NHIF, with registration ongoing nationwide. Kenyans are encouraged to enroll to access affordable healthcare services under the new scheme.

4 - Malaria Cases Drop in Western Kenya

Efforts to distribute insecticide-treated nets and improve access to antimalarial drugs have led to a significant reduction in malaria cases in Kisumu and surrounding areas, according to recent health reports.
